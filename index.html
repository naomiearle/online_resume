I believe that the language learning skills I developed through studying foreign languages in school and university has lead to my ability to pick up coding languages quickly.

I am currently looking for an entry-level or graduate position in Yorkshire as either a front-end or full-stack web developer. My principal aim with regards to my work at this stage is to improve my technical skills as much as possible and to gain experience in the industry and working with fellow developers as part of a creative team. In future, I am interested in becoming a lead developer and mentoring entry-level developers myself.
